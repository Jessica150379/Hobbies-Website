What is Afro-Latin Dancing

Afro Latin dancing is a term referring to the combining of African and/or Latin dance styles.
Hence, it is a sub category of fusion dance where two or more dances are combined.
These dance combinations use elements of both styles of dance mixed together to create a new look and feel.
